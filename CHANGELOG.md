# Kaasi Changelog

This document tracks all new features and major enhancements made leading up to the `v5.89k` release.

---

### 1. Light Mode & Custom Accent Colors
- **Description**: Introduced a comprehensive "Appearance" settings tab, allowing you to toggle between the classic Dark Mode and a crisp, high-contrast Light Mode. 
- **Accent Colors**: Added customizable accent color presets (Orange, Blue, Teal, Red, Purple, Green). The app's interface, charts, and interactive elements dynamically update to match your chosen theme and accent.

### 2. Category Exclusion Rules
- **Description**: Added a powerful new "Hidden Category Rules" panel in the Settings modal to help you manage how specific expense categories affect your dashboard. 
- **Features**: 
  - Hide specific categories from the main Dashboard Charts or Monthly Totals.
  - Exclude categories from PDF reports or Yearly Totals.
  - Dim hidden categories in your transaction lists for better visual filtering.

### 3. Settings UI Consolidation
- **Description**: Streamlined the Settings modal by merging the "Credit Card Settings" and "Accounts" tabs. Managing all your financial accounts is now centralized in one easy-to-use location.

### 4. Cash Account Visibility
- **Description**: You can now toggle the visibility of the default "Cash" account from the Settings panel or during Initial Setup, just like custom bank accounts.

### 5. Debts & Receivables Enhancements
- **Description**: Reordered the input fields in the Add/Edit Debt and Receivable modals to place the "Amount" field first, aligning with the standard transaction flow and improving data-entry speed.

### 6. Cash Counter Improvements
- **Description**: Enhanced the Cash Counter tool's inputs to strictly handle whole numbers, blocking accidental decimal entries. Added smooth scroll-wheel support for quickly increasing or decreasing bill counts.
