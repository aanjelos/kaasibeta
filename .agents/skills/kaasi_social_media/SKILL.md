# Kaasi Social Media & Content Strategy

This document outlines the brand voice, design aesthetics, and content pillars for Kaasi's social media presence (Instagram, LinkedIn, FB).

## 1. Brand Identity & Design Aesthetics
- **Visual Vibe:** Clean, minimalist, and app-centric. Modern and premium, not a boring corporate bank.
- **Colors:** The official brand colors are derived directly from the app's stylesheet:
  - **Primary Backgrounds:** Sleek dark grays/blacks (`#121212` for primary, `#1e1e1e` for secondary).
  - **Brand Accent (Kaasi Orange):** `#e67e26` (with hover states at `#d35400`).
  - **Text:** Crisp light grays (`#e0e0e0` for primary text, `#a0a0a0` for secondary).
  - **Semantic Values:** Green for income (`#27ae60`) and Red for expenses (`#e74c3c`).

## 2. Caption Style & Tone
- **Tone:** Conversational, witty, reassuring, and highly transparent. Emphasizes the "solo-developer" authenticity and uses relatable humor to tackle financial anxiety.
- **Core Message:** Heavy emphasis on privacy and being completely free (no ads, no paywalls, no hidden data harvesting).
- **Structure:** Short to medium length (3–6 sentences). Strong hook ➔ value/insight ➔ clear CTA (usually pointing to the blog).
- **Emojis:** Purposeful, not spammy (💸, 📉, 🚀, ✨, 🔒).
- **Hashtags:** Mix of functional and local (`#Kaasi`, `#ExpenseTracker`, `#PersonalFinance`, `#SriLanka`, `#Budgeting`, `#FinancialWellness`, `#SoloDev`).
- **Signature Sign-off:** *"Earn Well, and Spend Responsibly! 💸"*

## 3. Core Content Pillars
1. **🚀 App Updates & Features:** Teasers, launch announcements, and patch notes (e.g., v4 PDF reports, v5 Cloud sync updates).
2. **📚 Financial Literacy (Blog Promos):** Educational content addressing topics like managing inflation, the history of credit cards, and "how is it free?" to drive traffic to the blog.
3. **🛠️ "Build in Public" / Transparency:** Authentic updates from the solo developer. Sharing user milestones, web traffic surprises, and even deployment mistakes to build trust.
4. **🌴 Cultural & Seasonal:** Localized posts tying Sri Lankan holidays (like Avurudu and standard New Year) back to budgeting.

## 4. Examples of Past Captions
- **Educational / Engagement:** *"Famous last words (before your taxi gets cancelled): 'Cash or Card?' It feels like cards are everywhere, but here in Sri Lanka, a wild 90% of our daily spending is still cash... What about you? Are you trying to go cashless or do you prefer having notes in your wallet?"*
- **Promotional / Core Values:** *"Stop letting apps sell your financial data. 🔒 Kaasi is the 100% private, free, and powerful financial tracker built specifically for Sri Lanka (LKR). It runs entirely on your device. No sign-ups, no servers. Your data is yours alone..."*
- **Transparency / Status Update:** *"Well, this wasn't part of the plan. :( The v4 update unfortunately broke something during the deployment, so the site is temporarily offline while I sort it out. I'm working on it now and will have it back up as soon as possible. Thanks for your patience! 🛠️"*
- **Humor / Relatability:** *"Inflation has cooled, but the grocery bills clearly didn't get the memo. 📉 If you're stressed about where the money is going, the worst thing you can do is look away. Tracking turns 'I'm broke' into 'I spent 5k on snacks.' One is scary, the other is fixable..."*
